Who we are

The FleetMarket (www.thefleetmarket.com) is a Fleet sharing marketplace that facilitates people to rent trucks, trailers, tractors, and vans. We provide a highly innovative and modern platform to rent your fleet in 3 simple steps — Search, Book and Drive.

Our privacy policy has been compiled to address those concerned about how their their Personally Identifiable Information (PII) is used online. As defined in OMB Memorandum M-07-1616, PII refers to information that can be used to distinguish or trace an individual’s identity, either alone or when combined with other personal or identifying information that is linked or linkable to a specific individual. Kindly take the time to read our privacy policy to gain a clear knowledge of how we collect, use, protect or otherwise handle your PII in relation with our website.

What personal data we collect, how, and why we collect it

  • First and Last name
  • Mailing address
  • Phone number
  • Payment information such as but not limited to “bank card, credit card, etc”
  • Driver License or Commercial Drivers License Number

Cookies

The FleetMarket uses cookies (small, often encrypted, text files that are stored on your computer or mobile device) and similar technologies (“Cookies”) to provide the Services and help collect data. This policy section explains how we use Cookies to collect information about the way you use our Sites and Services, and how you can control them.

How We Use Cookies

We use Cookies to track how you use our Sites and Services by providing usage statistics. Cookies are also used to deliver Company information (including updates) and allow product authentication to you based upon your browsing history and previous visits to the Site. Information supplied to us using cookies helps us to provide a better online experience to our visitors and users and send marketing communications to them, as the case may be. Information supplied to us upon launching of the Services will enable the FleetMarket to manage your permissions, subscriptions, account type and improve the functionality of the Sites or Services.

While this information on its own may not constitute your “personal data”, we may combine the information we collect via Cookies with personal data that we have collected from you to learn more about how you use our Sites and Services to improve them.

Embedded content from other websites

In order for us to enhance our content and deliver a better online experience for our users, the FleetMarket may include embedded content such as videos, images, articles, social sharing icons etc. through the websites. Currently the FleetMarket uses and may use in the future contents from social media Sites such as Trustpilot, Facebook, LinkedIn and Twitter. Please note that cookies that might be presented to you from such sites are not controlled by us. And therefore we are not responsible for the security or privacy of any information collected by these third parties. We advice that you visit the individual privacy policy of these websites to have a better understand on how your Personal Identifiable Information is been used.

Social sharing icons are embedded throughout our Sites. Analytics

Google Analytics, a third-party provider, is used to collect information about Services usage and the users of the Services, including demographic and interest-level information. Google uses cookies in order to collect demographic and interest-level information and usage information from users that visit the Services, including information about the pages where users enter and exit the Services and what pages users view on the Services, time spent, browser, operating system, and IP address. How to Manage or Delete Cookies

Cookies are unique to each browser, therefore multi-browser users must manage their settings and preferences individually across all browsers. You can manage, restrict, clear or block cookies through your current web browser settings. Detailed instructions related to your current browser, can be found in the Help section of the browser.

For devices other than a desktop or laptop computer, users should refer to their instruction manual or utilize the help resource to acquire knowledge on how to manage cookies on that device.

Please note that, If you restrict, disable or block any or all Cookies from your web browser, mobile or other device, the Sites may not operate properly, and you may not have full access to the Services available through the Sites. The FleetMarket shall not be liable for any impossibility to use the Sites and Services or degraded functioning thereof, where such are caused by your settings and choices regarding cookies. If you have disabled one or more cookies, we may still use information collected from cookies prior to your disabled preference being set, however, we will stop using the disabled cookie to collect any further information.

Kindly visit www.allaboutcookies.org to learn more about cookies.

How we protect your information

We do not sell, trade or transfer your Personal Identifiable Information to third-parties. The FleetMarket takes the security of our users seriously. Our website is periodically scanned for security threats in order to make your experience as safe as possible. All this is done through a trusted company specialized in online security threats.

CHILDREN'S POLICY

Children Under Thirteen

The FleetMarket does not knowingly collect, either online or offline, personal information from persons under the age of thirteen. The required age to utilize the FleetMarket is twenty-one (21) years.

California Minors

While the Service is not intended for anyone under the age of 21, if you are a California resident who is under age 18 and you are unable to remove publicly-available content that you have submitted to us, you may request removal by contacting us at: privacy@thefleetmarket.com. When requesting removal, you must be specific about the information you want removed and provide us with specific information, such as the URL for each page where the information was entered, so that we can find it.

We are not required to remove any content or information that

  • Federal or State law requires us or a third party to maintain
  • is anonymized so that you cannot be identified
  • was not posted by you
  • you received compensation or other consideration for providing the Content or information
  • don’t follow our instructions for removing or requesting removal

Removal of your content or information from the Service does not ensure complete or comprehensive removal of that content or information from our systems or the systems of our service providers. We are not required to delete the content or information posted by you; our obligations under California law are satisfied so long as we anonymize the content or information or render it invisible to other users and the public.

CAN SPAM Act
